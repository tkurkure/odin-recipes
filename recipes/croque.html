Truffle Croque Monsieur

Created by Emmanuel Andrieu. Courtesy of Les Manoirs de Tourgéville

Description

A Normandy recipe to recreate at home, this Croque Monsieur combnes crusted toast, with melting cheese and the elegant taste of the truffle.

Ingredients

  • Slices of bread
  • 3 1/2 cups of grated Emmental Cheese
  • 2/3 cup of cream
  • Four slices of ham
  • Truffle oil and piece of truffle

Instructions

  1. Mix the cream and the cheese, sprinkle with pepper, and add a few drops of truffle oil. Store in the fridge overnight.
  2. Spread the mix on sliced bread and add a slice of ham with a few bits of truffle.
  3. Cover with another slice of bread and spread the mix of cheese and cream.
  4. Leave in an oven for 12 minutes at 350°F
