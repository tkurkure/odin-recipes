Cacio Cheese and Pepper Spaghetti

Created by Chef Fabio Cervo. Courtesy of Hotel Eden Rome

Description

This dish creates a stunning spaghetti infused with rosebuds. It is served at La Terazza, at the Hotel Eden Rome. (1 Michelin star)

Ingredients

  • 11 oz of spaghetti
  • 1 cup of light chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated cacio cheese
  • Pinch of edible rosebuds
  • Large pinch of black pepper of Madagascar
  • 2 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat chicken broth up to 60Â°C and add the rosebuds for 4 to 5 minutes
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add pepper (previously crushed under a mortar), and then add the broth
  3. Add the pasta, cooked al dente, to the sauce and keep on cooking on low
  4. Add pecorino cheese, extra virgin olive oil, and mix together
