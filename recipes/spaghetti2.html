Spaghetti with Ham, Ceps, and Black Truffle

Created by Christian Le Squer. Courtesy of Four Seasons.

Description

This innovative dish features a rich combination of flavors. It is served at Le Cinq, at the Four Seasons Hotel George V in Paris, France. (3 Michelin Stars)

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Prince de Paris ham
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Truffle
  • Cep mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Al dente spaghetti should be tossed in butter and parmesan, then laid out side by side to form the walls of the rectangle box.
  2. Glaze the ham, truffles, and cep mushrooms together with jus and place them in the timbale.
  3. Drizzle with truffled cream.
  4. Paint the exterior with more jus and top with a thin parmesan crust and delicately adorn with a gold leaf.
